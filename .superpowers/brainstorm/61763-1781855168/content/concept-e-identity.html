Institutional vocabulary

WC26 forecasting language

WC26 uses precise terminology derived from its forecasting methodology. These terms have specific definitions within the platform and differ from generic analytics language.

Model Health
Forecast Integrity
Composite indicator of calibration quality, reliability index, and model governance state. NOMINAL = ECE within gate, no challenger failures, no drift detected.
"Forecast Integrity NOMINAL — ECE 2.1% within 3.0% gate"
Verdict / Result chip
Forecast Grade
5-tier institutional grading system: Sharp (Brier <0.35) · Solid (0.35–0.55) · Close (0.55–0.75) · Miss (0.75–0.90) · Surprise (>0.90). Replaces binary hit/miss framing.
"England vs Serbia graded Surprise — Brier 0.941"
Calibration diagram
Confidence Surface
Reliability diagram showing predicted probability vs observed frequency across all settled prediction bins. A flat surface = perfectly calibrated confidence.
"Confidence surface shows alignment across 6 of 10 probability bins"
Open prediction / Pending
Operational Lock
A prediction lock that has been issued and is in-flight — awaiting match result for settlement. Count communicates operational pipeline workload.
"51 operational locks expire before Matchday 7"
Accuracy / Hit rate
Prediction Corpus
The complete set of locked predictions — graded and operational. "Corpus" emphasizes permanence: predictions cannot be added retroactively or edited after lock.
"Prediction corpus: 72 locks issued · 21 graded · 51 operational"
Biggest miss
Surprise Event
A settled prediction where the actual outcome fell in the bottom 15% of the model's probability distribution (Brier >0.90). Triggers pattern review.
"1 surprise event flagged: England vs Serbia"
Champion probability
Path Odds
Monte Carlo tournament path probability — probability each team wins the tournament across 10,000 simulations. Updated after each matchday settlement.
"Brazil path odds: 18.2% — unchanged from prior matchday"
Model change / Update
Forecast Drift
Meaningful shift in probability outputs without a model version change — caused by accumulating Elo evidence. Stable drift is expected and healthy. Sudden drift triggers review.
"Forecast drift: stable — no significant shift detected"
ECE / Error rate
Reliability Index
Composite calibration health indicator based on ECE reading vs gate (3.0% threshold). NOMINAL / WARNING / BREACH. Determines whether the confidence surface is trustworthy.
"Reliability Index NOMINAL — ECE 2.1% within gate"
Daily stats update
Intelligence Dispatch
Algorithmically generated daily briefing summarizing tournament state, model events, operational status, and notable variance. Appears at top of homepage each matchday.
"Intelligence Dispatch · June 19 · Brazil remains favorite at 18.2%"
